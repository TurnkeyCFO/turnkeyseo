FAQ

Direct answers before the first SEO call.

Strong SEO sites reduce friction by answering the questions buyers already have around scope, timing, price, and next steps. This page now does that without feeling cluttered.

Do you focus on local SEO or broader campaigns?

Turnkey SEO is primarily built around local visibility for Texas service businesses. Broader regional expansion can be supported, but local demand capture is the core lane.

Do I need a perfect website before I start?

No, but the current site condition affects the scope. The estimator includes website readiness because weak foundations usually increase setup work and slow results.

Do you offer one-time audits?

The buying path is audit-first even when the engagement becomes monthly. That keeps the first recommendation grounded in what the current search presence actually needs.

Why do you show pricing ranges instead of one flat monthly fee?

Because buyers want pricing logic, but local SEO scope still changes based on footprint, site condition, and add-ons like GBP or content support. The range keeps expectations clearer.

Can you help with Google Business Profile?

Yes. GBP optimization is one of the clearest local-growth levers for service businesses, and it can be scoped as an add-on or included in a broader monthly plan.

What happens after I reach out?

You get fit confirmation, likely package direction, and a clearer view of the audit layer before the strategy call. The next step is not left vague.
